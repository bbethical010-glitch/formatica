# Formatica: Android Operations Manual

Formatica for Android is a professional-grade media studio that brings powerful file processing tools to your mobile device with a stunning **Liquid Glass** interface. All operations are performed locally on your device to ensure maximum privacy.

---

## 🎨 Design: Liquid Glass
The Android version features the exclusive **Liquid Glass UI**, characterized by:
- **Depth & Transparency**: High-fidelity glassmorphism with dynamic mesh gradients.
- **Haptic Feedback**: Micro-interactions tailored for mobile touchscreens.
- **Adaptive Themes**: Seamless switching between Ethereal Dark and Clean Light modes.

---

## 🎬 Video Tools

### 🎵 Extract Audio
Strip audio tracks from video files quickly.
- **Output Formats**: MP3, AAC, WAV
- **Use Case**: Creating ringtones or saving music from video clips.

### 🎬 Convert Video
Change video formats for compatibility across different players.
- **Supported Outputs**: MP4, MKV
- **Bonus Feature**: Direct export to **Animated GIF**.

### 🗜️ Compress Video
Shrink large video files without significant quality loss.
- **Action**: Optimized for mobile processors to reduce storage footprint before sharing.

---

## 📄 Document & PDF Tools

### 📄 Convert Document
Transform document types for professional use.
- **Supported Formats**: DOCX, ODT, HTML
- **Note**: Ideal for reading desktop documents on the go.

### 🔗 Merge PDF
Combine multiple PDFs into a single file.
- **Action**: Pick multiple files from your Android storage and merge them instantly.

### ✂️ Split PDF
Break down large PDF reports.
- **Action**: Extract specific pages or split into smaller segments.

### 🌓 Greyscale PDF
Convert colored PDFs to black and white.
- **Benefit**: Reduces file size and prepares documents for eco-friendly printing.

---

## 🖼️ Image Tools

### 🖼️ Images to PDF
Turn your phone into a document scanner.
- **Action**: Select multiple photos from your Gallery and compile them into a paginated PDF.

### 🔄 Convert Image
Standardize your image library.
- **Supported Formats**: JPG, PNG, WEBP

---

## 🛡️ Privacy & Security
- **100% On-Device**: Formatica Android does not use cloud processing. Your sensitive documents and media never leave your internal storage.
- **Zero Tracking**: No telemetry or data collection in the core studio tools.

---

## Technical Specifications
- **Operating System**: Android 8.0 (Oreo) and above.
- **Storage Access**: Requires "Manage External Storage" permissions for certain PDF and video operations.
- **Engine**: Powered by high-efficiency local libraries including FFmpeg for mobile.
